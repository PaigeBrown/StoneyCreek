Interested in Becoming a Tenant?

Contact us for more information at 803-377-1013

Have you ever wanted to start your own small retail store? Here is a perfect, low-cost opportunity! Set up your space in the manner of your choosing to best display your wares. No need to set up and tear down each week; the flea market is indoors!

Only $33 per week to rent a 150 square foot section of the market. For new renters, pay for two weeks, and you get the next two free. This is for the first month only. After that, it is $33 per week.You can set up your site and leave it set up as long as you are renting the table. Since it is an indoor flea market, there is no need to remove your items for sale at the end of each day.
